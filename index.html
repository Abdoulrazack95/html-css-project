Silent Witness

Silent Witness is a BBC crime thriller series focusing on a team of forensic pathology experts and their investigations into various crimes.One of the most distinctive features of Silent Witness is its focus on the post mortem process. The pathologists spend much of their time in the Lyell Centre's laboratory, so a huge amount of effort is spent ensuring this aspect of the series is as intriguing and authentic as possible.

Crimes through the eyes of a team of forensic pathologists and forensic scientists

Silent Witness is a BBC crime thriller series focusing on a team of forensic pathology experts and their investigations into various crimes.
First broadcast in February 1996, and ongoing as of 2020.

The Origin

The original series was based on Professor Helen Witwell, a forensic pathologist based in Sheffield whom McCrery had known while serving as a police officer.

Main Characters

  • Nikki Alexander (Emilia Fox) – since Series 8.
  • Jack Hodgson (David Caves) – since Series 16.
  • Clarissa Mullery (Liz Carr) – Series 16–23.
  • Thomas Chamberlain (Richard Lintern) – Series 17–23.

Silencium

The theme music featured in the series is entitled Silencium and is performed by John Harle.The vocal section is performed by Sarah Leonard.
